# HIPAA Training Program

Organization: aLRELEVANT REGULATORS
Version: 1.0
Date: January 30, 2026
Owner: Compliance & Security

## Purpose
Provide mandatory HIPAA training for all personnel who access or manage PHI in CareDroid.

## Training Requirements
- Required for all staff with PHI access.
- Completed upon onboarding and annually thereafter.
- Training completion is documented and auditable.

## Training Modules

1. HIPAA Overview
   - Privacy Rule and Security Rule basics
   - Definitions of PHI and ePHI

2. Minimum Necessary Standard
   - Access only the PHI required for the task

3. Access Control and Authentication
   - Unique user IDs, MFA requirements
   - Password hygiene and secure sessions

4. Secure Communication
   - Approved channels for PHI
   - Prohibited tools and data-sharing practices

5. Incident Reporting
   - How to report suspected incidents
   - Response timeline expectations

6. Device and Workspace Security
   - Device encryption
   - Automatic lock requirements
   - Clean desk policy

7. Sanctions and Enforcement
   - Consequences for policy violations

## Assessment
- Minimum passing score: 80%
- Retraining required if assessment is failed

## Recordkeeping
- Training records retained for 6 years.

## Acknowledgement
Participants must acknowledge understanding and acceptance of HIPAA responsibilities.
