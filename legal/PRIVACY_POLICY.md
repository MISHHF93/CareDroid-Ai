# Privacy Policy for CareDroid-AI

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026  
**Effective Date:** January 31, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CareDroid-AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your protected health information (PHI). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

**HIPAA Compliance:** As a covered entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), we implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your PHI.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name, email address, phone number
- Date of birth, gender
- Account credentials (encrypted)
- Profile information

### 2.2 Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Medical history and conditions
- Medication records
- Laboratory results
- Clinical assessments (SOFA scores, drug interactions)
- Chat messages with medical content
- Emergency health information

### 2.3 Technical Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address and location data (when enabled)
- Usage analytics and app activity
- Crash reports and diagnostic data

### 2.4 Authentication Data
- Two-factor authentication tokens
- Biometric templates (stored locally on device, never transmitted)
- Login history and audit logs

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Treatment:** Provide AI-powered medical assistance and clinical decision support
- **Care Coordination:** Emergency escalation and 911 dispatch
- **Clinical Tools:** SOFA calculator, drug interaction checking, lab interpretation
- **RAG System:** Semantic search of medical knowledge base

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
- Account authentication and security (2FA, biometric)
- Audit logging and compliance monitoring
- Service improvement and bug fixes
- Fraud prevention and security
- Legal compliance and regulatory requirements

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell your PHI or personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Service Providers (Business Associates)
We share data with HIPAA-compliant service providers:
- **OpenAI** - AI language models (BAA in place)
- **Pinecone** - Vector database for RAG (BAA in place)
- **AWS/Google Cloud** - Infrastructure hosting (BAA in place)
- **Stripe** - Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Sentry** - Error tracking (anonymized)

All service providers sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Public health reporting
- Law enforcement (with valid legal process)
- Emergency situations (imminent harm)

### 4.4 Emergency Services
When you trigger emergency escalation, we share relevant health information with 911 dispatch centers to facilitate emergency response.

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256-GCM for database storage
- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 for all network communications
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- **Secure Authentication:** JWT tokens, OAuth2, 2FA/MFA

### 5.2 Access Controls
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with 22 permissions
- Principle of least privilege
- Multi-factor authentication required for privileged access
- Audit logging of all PHI access

### 5.3 Monitoring
- Real-time intrusion detection
- 24/7 security monitoring (Sentry, Prometheus)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Hash-chained immutable audit logs

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Automatically archived after 3 years of inactivity
- **Account Deletion:** Data deleted within 30 days of request (except legal holds)
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 7 years per HIPAA requirements
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups retained for 90 days

## 7. Your Rights Under HIPAA

You have the following rights regarding your PHI:

### 7.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of your PHI (within 30 days)

### 7.2 Right to Amend
Request corrections to inaccurate PHI

### 7.3 Right to Accounting
Request a list of disclosures (past 6 years)

### 7.4 Right to Restrict
Request restrictions on use/disclosure

### 7.5 Right to Confidential Communications
Choose how we contact you

### 7.6 Right to Breach Notification
Be notified of any PHI breaches within 60 days

### 7.7 Right to Delete
Request deletion of your account and data

**To exercise these rights, contact:** privacy@caredroid-ai.com
